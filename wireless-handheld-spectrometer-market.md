The FTRI (Fourier Transform Reflectance Infrared) spectrometers utilize infrared light to study the molecular composition of samples, while the NIR (Near Infrared) spectrometers examine the interaction of near-infrared light with materials. Lastly, the "Others" market encompasses handheld spectrometers using different technologies, such as UV-Visible or fluorescence, for varied analysis purposes.</p></p>
