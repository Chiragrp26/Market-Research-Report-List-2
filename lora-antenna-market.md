<p><p>The LoRa Antenna market can be categorized into two types: Indoor LoRa Antenna and Outdoor LoRa Antenna. Indoor LoRa Antennas are designed to be utilized within buildings, providing coverage within a limited range. They are typically smaller in size and have lower gain but are suitable for applications within a confined space. On the other hand, Outdoor LoRa Antennas are designed for outdoor use, providing wider coverage and higher gain. They are larger and more robust, capable of withstanding harsh weather conditions and offering extended range connectivity.</p></p>
